    Abstract

    Prithibeer Nakshatra is social organization working for the welfare and social upliftment of underprivileged children. We strive to provide education as one of our main objective . Food and healthcare for these children are also our point of focus . Our main driving force are none except todays youth .We are basically an youth biased social organization for the unprivileged kids registered under registration of society act . Non-Government Organization PRITHIBEER NAKSHATRA’s main centre of operation is situated in Durgapur. We recently expanded our scope of work to Kolkata and expect to expand throughout places of need soon will all your support.
